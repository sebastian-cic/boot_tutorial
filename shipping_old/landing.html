International Parcel Shipping From Alberta To Europe

Alberta's Affordable Parcle Shipping Company

For 30 years we have provided Calgarians with a low cost parcel courier service. We pride ourselves on customer service, reliability and low prices. Polimark Shipping now extends that service to all of Alberta!

Small Parcel Shipping

You can ship small parcels and packages with Polimark Air Service. Our minimum chargeable weight is now 1 kg to ship small parcels to most of our European destinations. Please use our price calculator to get a quote for shipping your package.

Polimark Shipping Home Pick Up

Polimark offers home pick up for your parcels and packages from anywhere in Alberta. This service is currently available by phone only, please contact us at 1-888-888-1186 for information.

Reliable and Safe shipping

With 30 years of experience you can rest assured that when you ship with Polimark your package will arrive at its destination safely and in a timely fashion.

By working with trusted partners in Ontario and Europe our parcels delivery service includes free insurance for $50 dollars on each parcel and additional insurance can be purchased. See our pricing details page for more information.
